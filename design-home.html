The Story

From Poverty
to Fine Art

My journey didn't start in a studio. It started with a dream bigger than my circumstances — a belief that beauty could exist even in the hardest places.

Every canvas I touch carries that story. Of faith. Of resilience. Of the conviction that art can change how we see the world and ourselves.

"I don't just create art. I create conversations, connections, and change."
